Profectus Estates are proud to present this newly constructed one bedroom self contained first floor flat located on Jerry's Lane in the popular area of Erdington. Within walking distance to Erdington train station and less than 10 minutes by car to the city centre this property is ideal for working professionals.The premises itself used to be the old Jerry's Inn public house and now has undergone a tremendous refurbishment and hence now is offering luxury self contained accommodation.
